# Commercial Use

VoxM is licensed for non-commercial use under the PolyForm Noncommercial License 1.0.0.

Commercial use is not included in the public license. Examples that may require a separate commercial license include:

- Using VoxM inside a company or paid service.
- Selling VoxM, a hosted VoxM service, or a modified VoxM build.
- Bundling VoxM into a commercial product.
- Offering VoxM-powered transcription or recording services to customers.
- Using VoxM as part of paid consulting delivery.

If you want to use VoxM commercially, contact the project maintainer to discuss a separate commercial license.

## Why This Model

The goal is to keep VoxM visible, inspectable, and useful for personal, educational, research, and non-commercial use while preserving a path to fund ongoing development.
